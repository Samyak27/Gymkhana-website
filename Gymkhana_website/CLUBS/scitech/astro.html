About

                   

The LNMIIT Astronomy Club was founded by Y10 Batch students: Rishabh Baid, Rajat Vyas, Samarth Mathur & Shasak Raina. The club aims to create awareness in the field of amateur astronomy by indulging in night-sky observations, talks & various competitions.

“Astronomy compels us to look upwards and leads us from this world to another.” - Plato
Astronomy is not limited to theoretical concepts but involves practical observation of what lies beyond our horizon. It questions the origin & existence of our universe. This keeps us driving & entices the desire to learn.

The club has successfully built an environment of healthy exchange of knowledge about astronomy & astrophysics. The dedicated team has made the college proud by winning various astronomical competitions held across the nation. We aim higher!

Achievements

During the session of 2015-2016, the club scaled new heights as students participated in various astronomical competitions across different colleges of India. Students proved their mettle by bagging the following positions in the techfests held at IIT’s:

• 2 teams participated in IIT BHU’s annual technical fest Technex and secured 2nd and 3rd position respectively.
• 1 team participated in IIT Delhi’s annual technical fest Tryst and secured 3rd position.
• 2 teams participated in IIT Kanpur’s annual technical fest Techkriti and secured 4th and 6th position respectively.
